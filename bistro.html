About Haji Makaan Bistro

A Modern Culinary Experience

Discover Our Bistro

Haji Makaan Bistro is our newest division, bringing you a fresh take on casual dining. We combine modern cooking techniques with quality ingredients to create dishes that are both familiar and exciting.

From our signature crispy fried chicken to gourmet burgers, wraps, and contemporary favorites, every dish is prepared fresh to order. Perfect for quick lunches, casual dinners, or late-night cravings - we're open 24x7 to serve you.

Our Signature Dishes

Taste the Difference

Crispy Fried Chicken

Golden crispy chicken pieces, perfectly seasoned and fried to perfection. Available in various sizes and spice levels.

Gourmet Burgers

Juicy burgers made with premium ingredients, fresh vegetables, and special sauces. A complete meal in itself.

Wraps & Sandwiches

Fresh wraps and sandwiches filled with your choice of fillings, vegetables, and sauces. Perfect for on-the-go.
