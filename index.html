About Us

Haji Makaan Restaurant

We serve authentic Arabic and Middle Eastern cuisine in Edapally, Kochi. Our menu features traditional dishes including Arabic Biriyani, Beef Rib Mandi, Al Faham Mandi, and more.

Popular Dishes

Beef Rib Mandi

Tender beef ribs slow-cooked with traditional spices, served with fragrant mandi rice.

Al Faham Mandi

Succulent grilled chicken marinated in special spices, served with aromatic mandi rice.

Arabic Biriyani

Fragrant basmati rice cooked with aromatic spices and tender meat, a signature dish.

Beef Dry Fry

Crispy and flavorful beef pieces, perfectly spiced and fried to perfection.
